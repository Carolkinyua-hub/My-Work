A story of Resilience

The year was 2014, while in the giant of Africa

I met a young lady whose purpose was to open my eyes to the wonderful world of coding

Fast foward to 2020, with 2 of my own I owned my path and took a leap of faith

However it wasn't to be a smooth path as anticipated , the juggling between minding my younglings and the impact of the pandemic, I caved

Choosing to fight stronger on another day

2021, she's back , though now on the third century , she is ready to slay the dragons
